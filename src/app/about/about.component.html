I'm Lucas Burns, a Full Stack Software Engineer in Portland, Oregon. I graduated from the University of Portland in May 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics. From November 2017 until February 2020, I worked at EVRAZ North America as a Full Stack Software Developer. There I built Java, C, and Javascript apps used throughout EVRAZ facilities. Since then, I've worked full time at Driveway.com (part of Lithia Motors) to create backend services for Driveway.com's pre-owned vehicle offerings in Kotlin and Spring. The majority of my work at Driveway.com has been in the context of inventory search, and it has been a great opportunity to try some of the more complex features of MongoDB. I've also greatly enjoyed learning to write GraphQL services as part of optimizing our search functionality since the potential performance benefits are quite noticeable.
